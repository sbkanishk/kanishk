§1 — About

Where proofs meet products.

I'm a third-year Integrated M.Sc. Applied Mathematics student at Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat. Somewhere between differential equations and debugging sessions, I found the thing I actually love: taking an abstract idea — a distribution, a dataset, a half-formed concept — and shaping it into something people can use.

I split my time between mathematical thinking and full-stack building. I've analyzed real-world datasets through the lens of statistics and optimization, shipped web projects end-to-end, and written long-form pieces breaking down the math behind everyday phenomena. Sustainability is a thread that runs through a lot of my project choices — I like problems where rigor and impact meet.

Outside of notebooks and codebases, I'm usually watching football, deep in a Marvel timeline debate, or mapping out Westerosi family trees for fun. I'm always building something, and always learning the next thing that lets me build it better.
